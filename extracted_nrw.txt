Impact of Reducing Non-Revenue Water (NRW)
on Energy Costs
Water utilities consume 0.4–0.8 kWh of energy per m³ of pumped water.
Every m³ of NRW saved reduces energy consumption proportionally.
NRW reduction lowers pumping hours, pressure demand, and treatment costs.
Energy represents 25–40% of total water utility operational costs.
Example: Reducing 50,000 m³/year saves ~27,500 kWh → ~4,100 €/year.
